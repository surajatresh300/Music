What is Music?

Music: A Universal Language

Music is the art of arranging sounds to create harmony, melody, and rhythm. It transcends language barriers and connects people worldwide.

Core Elements of Music

Rhythm

Rhythm is the pattern of beats or the timing in music. It gives music its structure and keeps all elements in sync.

Melody

Melody is the sequence of musical notes that is most recognizable in a piece. It's what makes a tune memorable.

Harmony

Harmony is the combination of different musical notes played together to enhance the melody, creating a fuller sound.
